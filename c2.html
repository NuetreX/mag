TRIWIZARD CUP

Where Minds Clash and Prowess Unleashed—Decode, Compete, Conquer!

Embark on a thrilling journey of intellect and physical prowess with TRIWIZARD CUP— an event designed to test the mettle of students from all streams. Your epic quest begins as a team of dynamic duos, kick off the excitement by choosing a mind-bending puzzle from the starting point. The challenge lies in decoding this puzzle to unveil the key answer strategically placed on marked tables. Upon successful unraveling, teams dive into a series of competitions to showcase their prowess. Join us at TRIWIZARD CUP for an unforgettable fusion of mental ability and teamwork, where every puzzle solved is a step closer to victory. May your wits be sharp, and may the spirit of competition guide you through your quest. The ultimate test awaits—let the games begin!
Eligibility criteria- ● Open to all students.

TEAM SIZE- 4 members in each team

EVENT LEVELS:

*DOODLE MAPS will be provided in the starting point to each team.
-FRONT OF THE MAP- GARDEN OF EGGS
-BACK OF THE MAP-CAMPUS LAYOUT

LEVEL 1: (PREFERRED VENUE : STUDENTS SQUARE GARDEN)

● A Jigsaw puzzle will be placed inside the golden egg.
● After solving, come back to the starting point with a snapshot.
● First 20 teams which manage to solve the puzzle will immediately move on to the next round.

LEVEL 2: (ENTIRE CAMPUS)

● The first 20 teams will be given an opportunity to draw the words from a bowl.
● Each team has to guess the correct words and reach the designated location in accordance with the word.
● The location can be any place inside the campus.
● After reaching the given location, they will have to figure out the next location in accordance to the word given to them at the reached location.

LEVEL 3:

● In the final round, the participants will be provided with questions wherein each question they
answer will give them access to a bullet.
● In front of them is a cup pyramid, in which they have to make each cup fall off with the bullets gained by answering as many questions required.
● Once the team is done with destroying the cup pyramid, they have to run toward the triwizard cup.

WINNING CRITERIA:

● The first team who picks up the Triwizard cup first will be the winner followed by the first and the second runner up.

EVENT DURATION: 2 hours

Registration fee: 250/- per team

NOTE: Each team will be provided with a participation certificate

*For all Non-Technial events contact Engineering or department representatives

Rules

Rules During the Event: ● No usage of gadgets during the event, including smartphones or other electronic devices.
● Teams must adhere to the designated pathways and areas, respecting campus rules. Snapshot Submission:
● Teams must provide a clear snapshot of the solved puzzle in Level 1 for verification.
● Falsifying snapshots or attempting to bypass verification will result in disqualification.
