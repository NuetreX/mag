Business Quiz

Business quiz is all about the thirst for unquenchable knowledge. It is a fun and educative way to test your knowledge about the nuances of business. You will be challenged with critical questions that hone your quizzing skills and shape your minds with logical analysis and carefully curated reasoning.

REGISTRATION FEE: 750/-

Team Size: 2 members

Rules

1. Participants should adhere to the institute's code of conduct and discipline.
2. The competition will be taking place over the period of 2 days, i.e. the entirety of MAGNOVITE. In case a contestant fails to appear for the competition on any day, the contestant will be automatically disqualified.
3. The medium of language throughout this fest will remain English.
4. Any malpractice during the competition or misbehaviour towards volunteers or judges will lead to disqualification.
5. All participants must have their phones, laptops, chargers, pen drives, dongles, etc. Campus Wi-Fi will be available to the participants. In case of unforeseen circumstances, however, participants are expected to have their hotspots ready for connectivity purposes.
6. Participants are requested to be in formal attire (carry blazers if needed).
7. Participants are requested to report at least 45 minutes before the commencement of their respective events.
8. All participants are expected to carry their college-given ID cards at all times.
9. Make sure you save the event POCs contact information throughout the event.
10. Any doubts regarding the rounds should be cleared prior to the commencement of the round. Round POCs will be available for consultation throughout the round.
11. In case of any emergency, please let the POCs know as soon as possible.
12. The decision of the coordinators and judges will be final. In case of any discrepancies,the same shall be followed.
13. The POCs have a right to change the time and venue of the events at any given time owing to allotments. The same will be communicated to the participants through a chat group.
14. The committee has the right to change the time and venue of the events and participants will be informed of the same via a group chat for their respective events.
15. There would be no refund provided if the participants back out.
