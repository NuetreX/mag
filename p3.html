Quick Flicks: Live Your TV Fantasies, Be the Star!

Do you absolutely adore TV shows? Have you ever daydreamed about stepping onto the screen and becoming a part of your favourite series? Well, here’s your golden ticket to making that dream a reality! Welcome to Quick Flicks, where you not only watch the shows but become the star of your very own channel. It’s like stepping into the TV universe and living out your wildest fantasies. Are you a fan of crime-solving detectives? Put on that detective hat and become Sherlock Holmes for a day. Do you love the drama of historical epics? Dress up as a gallant knight or a powerful queen and transport yourself to a different era. Are you enchanted by the world of fantasy? Embrace your inner wizard or warrior and embark on a magical journey. So, if you’re ready for an unforgettable experience where you get to be both the audience and the star of your favourite TV channel, Quick Flicks is where the magic happens. Join us for a day of fun, creativity, and a whole lot of TV-inspired adventure!

REGISTRATION FEE: 250/-

Team Size: 5-5 members

Rules

1. Each team will be given a total of 10 minutes in which the judge can change the channels for a set amount of time (5–6 times). Participants cannot switch channels.
2. Participants cannot interchange the channels between themselves, and doing so will result in negative marking.
3. Any usage of inappropriate language, gestures, or vulgar terms that do not adhere to the university's regulations will lead to immediate disqualification.
4. Participants can bring their own appropriate props, costumes, etc. that match the CHRIST (Deemed to be University) dress codeand test your limit state!
