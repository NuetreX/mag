Battle of Bands

Sound Clash: Unleash the Beats!

Rock the stage at our 'Battle of the Bands' event! Calling all music enthusiasts to showcase their talent and compete for the title. Bring your best beats, electrifying solos, and a whole lot of energy. It's a showdown of sound you won't want to miss!

REGISTRATION FEE: 750/-

Team Size: 4-10 members

Rules

1. Participant teams must stick to ‘Fusion’ genre of music.
2. No more than four teams are allowed per college.
3. The number of participants per team should be between 4 to 10.
4. Time limit: 8+3 min (3 minutes for soundcheck). Exceeding the time limit will result in a point reduction.
5. One individual is allowed to be a part of only one band. (Playing for more than one band will result in immediate disqualification).
6. Any regional languages, as well as English songs, will be permitted 7. Original compositions are encouraged.
8. Explicit or offensive lyrics/gestures/misconduct will be disqualified immediately.
9. Synthesized beats or prerecorded music are not allowed.
10. Participants are ought to bring their own instruments. Only a drum kit, microphones, and amplifiers will be provided.
11. The decision of the judges will be final.
