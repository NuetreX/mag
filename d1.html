REVIVAL OUTLOOK

The Department of Architecture presents to you Revival Outlook, It is a Fashion Design competition. Revival Outlook “Creating a Timeless Fashion”- (Flagship Event)The theme of the event is "Underwater Utopia", The participants' clothing must evoke fluid silhouettes that mimic underwater architecture and marine life movement, embodying the spirit of "Underwater Utopia" with flowing, shimmering pieces in oceanic hues. To round off this alluring motif, suggest accessories modeled after sea life and architectural details, as well as makeup and haircuts that exude fluidity and enchantment.

Eligibility Criteria

● Participants should be from any UG course.

Suggested Venue and Time

● Block 1 Auditorium
● Day 2: 2:00 pm to 5: 00 pm
● 4 classrooms preferably in the same block for changing
1st round- RAMP WALK
2nd round- PRESENTATION and THE JUDGES CRITS

Judgment Criteria

Creativity: 15 points -Execution of theme in Design: 15 points -Use of materials and Craftsmanship: 10 points -Composition and Presentation: 10 points A total of 50 POINTS. The decisions of the judges will be considered final and irrevocable. Wishing all the participants the very best

REGISTRATION FEE: 750/-

Team Size: 4-8 members

Rules

Time duration: 4 hours; Group event.
● There can be a minimum of 4 Members and a maximum of 8 members on a team.
● Each team should have a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of 6 members to model the attire.
● The theme of the event is "Underwater Utopia", The participants' clothing must evoke fluid silhouettes that mimic underwater architecture and marine life movement, embodying the spirit of "Underwater Utopia" with flowing, shimmering pieces in oceanic hues. To round off this alluring motif, suggest accessories that are modeled after sea life and architectural details, as well as makeup and haircuts that exude fluidity and enchantment.
● The participants should plan beforehand and bring the necessary materials and outfits.
● Ready-to-wear clothes will not be accepted.
● The use of recyclable and sustainable materials will be given preference.
● In any way, the designed clothes should not be revealing.
● If choosing sleeveless or above-knee-length outfits, the performers must wear a complete body covering underneath.
● The judgment of the jury panel will be final.
● Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
● The event will take place on campus.
