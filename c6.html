The Gentleman Burglar

Technical Prowess Meets High-Stakes Intrigue!

Embark on an exhilarating journey with us at "The Gentleman Burglar" technical event. Immerse yourself in a captivating simulation that draws inspiration from the intrigue of a high-stakes bank heist. Your technical prowess will be challenged and refined through three compelling rounds, promising an experience like no other. Join us for an unforgettable adventure where your skills will be the key to unlocking success!

Eligibility criteria-

● Any student with knowledge of coding and circuit building
● Teams consisting of 2-4 members can participate in the event
Round 1-Decryption
Time limit-40 minutes
● Setting-The robber has left an encrypted message and the teams have to decrypt it using Python or C to find the robber's details.
● Teams use Python or C for decryption.
● Points will be awarded based on how fast teams decrypt the message.
● No elimination in this round.
● Teams will get more time for round 2 if they decrypt the message before 30 minutes.
Round 2-Debugging
Time limit-30 minutes
● Setting-The security system of the bank has a bug, teams have to debug the circuit.
● Points will be awarded based on how fast the teams debug the circuit.
● Elimination will take place based on the time taken by teams in the 1st and 2nd round.
Round 3-Circuit building
Time limit-45 minutes
● Setting-The bank requires a new security system
● Teams are to design a new circuit for the bank security using Tinkercad software or Multisim.
● Teams have to describe their circuit to the members of the bank(The judges).
● Winners will be chosen based on time taken and the design of the circuit.

REGISTRATION FEE: 300/-

Team Size: 2-4 members

Rules

● Usage of phones will not be permitted during the event.
● Every team member must carry an ID card
● Time limits should be maintained , if exceeded the team will be directly eliminated.
● Teams are required to know how to decrypt encrypted data.
● Every team must have a laptop with Python IDE , codeblocks or Dev C++ installed.
● Every team must use the online simulation software “TinkerCAD” for round 3.
● Teams can only use their laptops during the first & third round.
● Teams will not be allowed to discuss with other teams.
● Teams cannot refer to any material or sources during the event.
● The judge's decision will be final.
