Ad-Venture

Event Name

Step into a world where selling lightsabers to Spartans or a magic wand to Batman isn’t just a fantasy. Welcome to Ad-Venture 2023, where your selling skills will be put to the ultimate test! Can you make the impossible sale? In this unique and challenging Shark Tank-inspired event, each team will have a random product and an extraordinary audience to sell. Your task? Craft a pitch that leaves everyone in awe. It’s a battle of wits and creativity in a single-round showdown. May the most persuasive entrepreneur emerge victorious.

REGISTRATION FEE: 500/-

Team Size: 3-4 members

Rules

1. The topics will be given two days prior to the event.
2. Participants must stick to the product and fictional audience given to them during the presentation.
3. Participants may use any mode of presentation to sell their product (PPTs, props, printouts, skits, etc.)
4. Participants will be given a total of 3 minutes for their pitch and 2 minutes for rebuttal with the judges following the pitch presentation. Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative markings.
5. Any usage of inappropriate language, vulgar terms, or dark humour that does not adhere to the CHRIST (Deemed to be University) regulations will lead to immediate disqualification.
