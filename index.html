MAGNOVITE 2024 will be conducted offline at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Kengeri Campus on February 12th & 13th . Fest is open to Undergraduate & Postgraduate students of all disciplines and multiple teams from the same university are allowed to participate in the fest. The fest is a national inter-collegiate competition and is not open to CHRIST (Deemed-to-be University) students. All participants are required to carry their respective college ID cards and valid ID proof. Online registrations in accordance can be done via the registration link provided in the email or at magnovite.christuniversity.in. The participants can register individually or as contingent. The individual registration fees are mentioned for each event in the brochure.
