Trivia Triumph Tournament

Riddles, Puzzles, Team Thrills!

It is a series of exciting rounds with fun questions and puzzles and riddles for teams to solve together. Multiple rounds will be played with riddles, puzzles and fun questions.
Teams should have 3 people.

REGISTRATION FEE: 250/-

Team Size: 3 member

Rules

1. It is a group event
2. A team should have 3 participants.
3. There will be various rounds and the scoring of all the rounds in the end will decide the winner.
4. Rounds will include fun games and puzzles and group activities.
