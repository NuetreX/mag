Pareidolia

Pareidolia: Your Inner World, Expressed in Symbols.

Pareidolia is a transformative exploration of your inner world. Through thought-provoking prompts and the expressive power of symbolism, this event delves into the profound depths of the psyche. Armed with paper and drawing materials, participants will translate their thoughts and emotions into symbols, each carrying profound metaphorical meaning. Participants then unlock the hidden narratives of the human mind, revealing layers of emotions, thoughts, and personal stories by depicting the narrative with the help of symbols. “Pareidolia” fosters community and empathy as all participants share their symbolic narratives with others. It is a unique experience where symbols become the language of the soul, and self-discovery is an art form.
Individual Event

REGISTRATION FEE: 250/-

Team Size: 1 member

Rules

1. This is an individual event.
2. Participants must use the provided prompts as the foundation for their symbolic narratives. These prompts are designed to inspire creativity and introspection.
3. Each participant is limited to a maximum of six symbols in their narrative. This limitation encourages concise and meaningful expression while maintaining the challenge of conveying complex ideas through symbolism.
4. A time period of 1 hour would be given to all the participants to prepare their stories and symbols.
5. Each participant will get 4-5 minutes to present their narratives to the judges. Participants should stick to the time limit
