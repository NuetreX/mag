INNOVATIVE CAD DESIGNER

Welcome to the INNOVATIVE CAD DESIGNER competition hosted by the Department of Civil Engineering and Architecture. We're excited to invite participants from diverse academic backgrounds to unleash their creativity and technical skills in computer-aided design.

Event Details:

Eligibility Criteria:
- Open to B.Tech in Civil Engineering.
- M.Tech students (Any Civil Engineering Branch) are invited to take on an additional challenge.

Design Challenge:
- Regardless of academic level, participants will create a double-line architectural plan, front elevation, and sectional elevation based on a given single-line diagram.

Difficulty Levels:
- We've designed the event to be inclusive for everyone, offering a standard difficulty level suitable for B.Tech and B.Arch students.
- M.Tech students are encouraged to explore additional complexities at their own pace.

Time Duration:
- 2 Hours

Team Size:
- 1-2 Member

Registration Fee:
- Rs300

Evaluation Criteria:
Creativity in Design:
- Showcase your innovative design solutions, regardless of academic background.
Precision in Architectural Plans:
- We value attention to detail and accuracy in architectural plans. Clarity in Front and Sectional Elevations: - Present your design elements clearly in both front and sectional elevations.
Efficient Use of AutoCAD Tools:
- Demonstrate effective use of AutoCAD tools for successful design creation. Adherence to Given Information:
- Align your designs with the provided single-line diagram, maintaining a high level of detail.

Prizes and Recognition:
- Winners will be recognized for their outstanding design skills.
- Certificates of achievement will be awarded to all participants.

Important Note:
The organizers reserve the right to modify any event rules or guidelines.

Additional Levels:
Certainly! Here's a more formal and concise rephrasing:


Level 1:

- Participants will receive specifications one week in advance.
- Followed by presentations in front of judges.

Level 2:

- Participants are allotted 2 hours for on-campus design presentations before judges.

Level 3:

- Participants are tasked with creating a physical model based on their design.

Rules

1. Design Complexity: - Explore creative elements within a standard difficulty level. - M.Tech students can dive into additional complexities at their discretion.
2. Submission Requirements: - Submit your designs within the specified time frame.
3. Collaboration: - Embrace collaboration between participants of different courses for cross-learning.
4. Tool Mastery: - Showcase competency in using AutoCAD features, tailoring your approach to your academic background.
