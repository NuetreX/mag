How I Met Your Killer?

Decode the Clues, Unleash Your Inner Sleuth

Come on, who doesn’t want to be the next Patrick Jane? Go around, observe people, look for clues, and find out who the killer is with absolute finesse. This event is just for that. Here, you’ll be profiling and interrogating your suspects to make a solid theory on who the plausible killer would be. “It’s elementary, my dear Watson.”

REGISTRATION FEE: 500/-

Team Size: 3-4 members

Rules

1. The event will consist of 3 rounds- Preliminary Round, Clue Collection Round and the Murder Mystery Round.
2. The Preliminary Round will consist of a worksheet which will include puzzles and trivia. The top teams will move to the clue collection round.
3. The Clue Collection Round will consist of a mini treasure hunt in a designated area and the top teams will qualify for the Murder Mystery Round.
4. AThe final round is based on the idea of a killer who is on the loose, and in order to find the killer, the teams will be provided with a set number of suspects, whom they will have to interrogate and make a profile. In the end, each team will have to come up with a theory, describing who they think is the actual killer.
5. All teams will be given a total time of 30 minutes (Subject to Change) to interrogate all the suspects. The teams are free to use this time at their own discretion.
6. All participants are free to discuss their ideas about the ‘case’ once they are done with their interrogations.
7. No electrical gadgets are allowed during the interrogations. Participants are allowed to take down notes with pen and paper.
8.No discussion is permitted, apart from the discussions with your own teammates.
- Total time for interrogation: 30 min (subject to change)
- Time for profiling and making the theory: 20 min
- Time for presenting to judges: 5 min+2 min
