TERRABSTRAC

TERRABSTRAC

TerrAbstract i.e. “Creating Abstract using clay” Creative art has to be made out of the provided spilled inked sheets for all the participants.

Eligibility Criteria

● Participants should be from any UG course.


Registration

● Registration amount: 500/- per group.
● Each participant must register via the Google form.

Venue, Date and Time Duration

● Art and Workshop space in the basement of Crystal Block
● Day 1 - 2 pm - 5 pm

Judging Criteria

● Handling of materials (10 points)- How well the participants use the clay provided.
● Relevance to Theme (10 points)- adherence to theme by participants
● Creativity (10 points)
● Level of Abstraction (20 points)

REGISTRATION FEE: 500/-

Team Size: 3 members

Rules

Rules and Regulations
Time duration: 3 hours
Group event: 3 per group
● The topic for abstraction will be given on spot.
● Participants have to create an abstract model using clay which will be provided. No extra clay will be given to any individual.
● Clay and essential tools will be provided to the participants.
● Participants may bring their own tools; if necessary.
● Any type of mold will not be allowed in the competition.
● The judgment of the jury panel will be final.
● Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
● The event will take place on campus.
