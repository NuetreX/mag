Enigma Redesigned

Unlock the Code, Master the Challenge!

The Cryptic Code Challenge, hosted by the Department of Computer Science, introduces three captivating rounds tailored to test participants' problem-solving prowess.
In Round 1, 'Cipher Quest,' contestants unravel encrypted messages using diverse cryptographic techniques to unveil crucial clues pivotal for progression.
Round 2, 'Debugging Blitz,' demands swift thinking as participants rectify erroneous code snippets to extract essential hints.
The climactic Round 3, 'Algorithmic Enigma,' presents intricate coding puzzles, culminating in unlocking a digital vault.
Join us for this exhilarating fusion of cryptanalysis and programming finesse, where the ultimate victory awaits the astute minds who unravel the final code phrase.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

Participants from any UG course

EVENT LEVELS:

1. Round 1: Cipher Quest
Participants delve into a series of encrypted codes or ciphers, employing various cryptographic techniques (like Caesar cipher, substitution cipher, or binary/hexadecimal conversions) to unravel hidden clues crucial for advancement to the next round.
2. Round 2: Debugging Conundrum
Participants face a challenge of fixing buggy code snippets within a limited timeframe. Each resolved bug unveils vital clues or components of a code necessary to progress further.
3. Round 3: Algorithmic Enigma (Final Round)
The ultimate challenge features a locked digital vault protected by a complex password. Participants encounter programming challenges presented as riddles or partially coded snippets, requiring solutions related to algorithms, data structures, or logical puzzles to reveal the pivotal keyword or code phrase necessary for unlocking the vault.

EVENT DURATION:

Round 1: Cipher Quest: 45 minutes - 1 hour
● Participants decrypt a concise series of encrypted codes or ciphers within this time frame.
Round 2: Debugging Conundrum: 45 minutes - 1 hour
● Teams swiftly receive and fix a limited number of buggy code snippets within the given time.
Round 3: Algorithmic Enigma (Final Round): 1 - 1.5 hours
● This round involves solving a compact set of programming challenges presented as riddles or partially coded snippets to unlock the digital vault.

Overall Event Duration: Approximately 2.5 - 3.5 hours

REGISTRATION FEE: 300/-

TEAM SIZE: 2-4 members

NOTE : Each team will be provided with a participation certificate.

Rules

1. Team Composition: Teams should consist of a maximum of 4 members. Collaboration beyond the registered team is strictly prohibited.
2. Decryption Protocols: Participants must utilize only the provided encrypted messages for decryption. Any external decryption tools or unauthorised assistance are strictly prohibited.
3. Cryptographic Techniques: Teams are allowed to employ various cryptographic techniques such as Caesar ciphers, substitution ciphers, binary/hexadecimal conversions, etc., to decode the encrypted messages.
4. Ethical Conduct: Respectful and ethical conduct is expected from all participants. Any form of unsportsmanlike behaviour or attempts to sabotage other teams will result in immediate disqualification.
5. Use of AI and Cheating: The use of AI or any form of cheating, plagiarism, or unauthorized external assistance in deciphering the messages is strictly forbidden. Teams found violating this rule will face immediate elimination from the competition.
6. Submission Guidelines: Solutions to the decrypted messages must be submitted within the specified time limit and in the required format outlined by the organizers.
7. Event Integrity: The organizing committee reserves the right to amend rules, disqualify teams, or take any necessary action to maintain the integrity and fairness of the competition.
8. Fair Competition: The Cryptic Code Challenge aims to foster an environment where ingenuity, problem-solving skills, and ethical conduct prevail. The rules are designed to ensure fair competition while encouraging creativity and strategic thinking among participants.
